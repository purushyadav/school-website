About Us

Welcome to Sri Venkateshwara E.M School , a premier educational institution located beside the government hospital in Palukur village, Banaganapalli mandal, Nandyal district. Our school is dedicated to providing a nurturing and stimulating learning environment for our students.

Under the guidance of our esteemed Correspondent, Mr. G. Ramapullaiah, we strive to cultivate a love for learning and encourage our students to reach their full potential. Our dedicated faculty members are committed to fostering the holistic development of each student, equipping them with the skills and knowledge necessary for success in an ever-changing world.

At Sri Venkateshwara E.M School , we believe in fostering a strong sense of community and values such as respect, integrity, and perseverance. Our students are encouraged to explore their interests, develop critical thinking skills, and become responsible global citizens. Join us on this journey of academic excellence and personal growth at Sri Venkateshwara E.M School, where every student is valued and empowered to succeed.

School Life

Motivational classes

Motivational classes at Sri Venkateshwara E.M School are designed to inspire and empower students to reach their full potential. These classes focus on building self-confidence, setting goals, overcoming obstacles, and developing a positive mindset. Through interactive sessions, guest speakers, and activities, students are encouraged.

Yoga Classes

At Sri Venkateshwara E.M School, we understand the importance of physical and mental well-being for our students. That's why we offer regular yoga classes as part of our curriculum. These classes are designed to help students improve their flexibility, strength, and focus while reducing stress and promoting overall well-being.

Cultural fest

The cultural fest at Sri Venkateshwara E.M School is a vibrant and colorful celebration of diversity and creativity. It provides students with a platform to showcase their talents in various forms such as music, dance, drama, art, and more. The fest is a melting pot of different cultures, traditions, and languages, allowing students to learn from each other.

Careers

Teaching Staff

The teaching staff at Sri Venkateshwara E.M School is a dedicated and passionate group of educators who are committed to providing a high-quality education to their students. They are experienced professionals who bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the classroom, inspiring and motivating students to reach their full potential. Transportation

Sri Venkateshwara E.M School provides transportation services for students who require it. The school has a fleet of buses that are safe, reliable, and well-maintained. The buses are equipped with all necessary safety features and are driven by experienced and licensed drivers who prioritize the safety of the students. The transportation services cover a wide area, ensuring that students from various locations can easily access the school.
